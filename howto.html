WAP Coin Buying Guide

We're beyond excited for you to join the WAP Coin family by investing in WAP Coin. Especially if this is your first time buying on the Binance Smart Chain or a token for that matter, we know this can be intimidating. Don't worry we made this page to help guide you through buying WAP Coin the right way.

PancakeSwap

Steps

  1. Using your mobile wallet browser, or desktop browser, navigate to PancakeSwap.
  2. Click the check mark to accept WAP as your output currency and Continue.
  3. Click on the settings icon (to the right of the text, "Trade tokens in an instant") and set the Slippage tolerance to anywhere from 10% - 15%. Exit the settings popup.
  4. Enter the amount of BNB you wish to swap.
  5. Approve the transaction in your wallet and celebrate!
